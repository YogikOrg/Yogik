Yogik – Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Yogik ("the app") is developed and maintained by YogikOrg. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Collect

Yogik does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no account system, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connection requirement.

Data Stored on Your Device

All settings and session configurations you create within the app (such as timer settings, breath ratios, custom sequences, and voice preferences) are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults and file storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Yogik does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services.

Children's Privacy

Yogik does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be published at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@yogikapp.com.
