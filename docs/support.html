Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there no sound during a session?

Yogik uses the audio playback category, so sound works even on silent mode. Make sure your device volume is turned up. If the issue persists, close and reopen the app.

How do I reset a saved timer or sequence?

Swipe left on any saved item in the list to delete it, or simply overwrite it by saving new settings with the same configuration.

Can I use Yogik without an internet connection?

Yes. Yogik works entirely offline. No internet connection is required at any time.

How do I import a sequence shared by someone else?

Tap the .yogikseq file on your device (from AirDrop, Messages, or email) and it will open directly in Yogik.
